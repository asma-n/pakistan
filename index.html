About

Pakistan is a beautiful and amazing country. From Karachi to Lahore, it is a land of great diversity. Pakistan shares its borders with India, Iran, and China. Moreover, it has many tourist attractions such as Kashmir and Baltistan that are worth seeing. There are many reasons to visit Pakistan such as the weather, cultures, cost of living and so on. Weather in Pakistan ranges from hot summers to cold winters. A beautiful country, Pakistan is known for its breathtaking landscapes, as well as its unique traditions and culture. Take in the sights of the Himalayas (the second highest mountain range in the world) and explore the Tribal Areas, before visiting some of Pakistan's wonderful ancient archaeological sites. There are also plenty of exciting cities to visit, including those along the Indus River and Karachi.
